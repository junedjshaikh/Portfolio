FurniShop — Furniture Shopping App
UI/UX Design  |  Figma  |  Android & iOS  |  Internship
// PROJECT OVERVIEW
FurniShop is a furniture shopping mobile application designed to make browsing and purchasing furniture simple and visually engaging. I designed the complete user experience from the ground up, beginning with wireframes to define the structure and user flow, then moving into high-fidelity UI design in Figma. The project included onboarding screens, home page, product listing, product detail pages, wishlist, cart management, checkout flow, and user profile. A full design system was created with reusable components, typography rules, spacing system, and color variants for both light mode and dark mode to maintain consistency across Android and iOS. The final design was handed over to the development team and the application was successfully launched for production use on both Play Store and App Store.
